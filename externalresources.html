At Crescendo Green, we believe that successful forestry and restoration projects are built on sound science, practical experience and knowledge sharing. Below is a selection of freely available publications and resources from leading research institutions and international organisations that we regularly recommend to clients, partners and practitioners.

Melia volkensii (Mukau)

Published by the Kenya Forestry Research Institute (KEFRI) in collaboration with JICA, this practical manual covers propagation, nursery management, field establishment, pruning, pest management and harvesting of Melia volkensii.
